## What is Netuno?

It's a **low-code** **open-source**, and completely free platform.

Our main goal is to boost your programming career by providing total independence and autonomy through mastery of *fullstack* development.

The platform promotes *fullstack* development by demystifying the *backend*. It significantly simplifies several layers in the development of:

* Web solutions and applications;
* *Backoffice* systems;
* REST APIs.

By understanding the *fullstack* ecosystem, you will discover whether your natural aptitude is more geared towards the *frontend* or the *backend*. You will also become a much more capable professional, able to interact, communicate, and collaborate with teams from different specialties.

## Prerequisites to get started

With Neptune, the learning curve is eased by the removal of several common technical barriers, but the human factor—your creativity and intelligence—remains essential.

Before beginning your studies, it is crucial to align your expectations and ensure that you have:

* **Willingness to step outside your comfort zone:** Continuous learning is part of the journey in technology.
* **Interest and patience:** Learning new technologies and concepts requires time, practice, and dedication.
* **Focus and persistence:** Software development requires a high level of concentration. Challenges will arise, but persistence is the key to success.
* **Desire for optimization:** A desire to produce robust solutions more quickly and efficiently.

:::tip Tips and Best Practices
* **Don't give up in the face of difficulties:** It's common for learning to indirectly develop your resilience. Many give up when faced with obstacles, but remember: if software development were extremely easy, it would already be fully automated. Your human reasoning is indispensable.
* **Ask for help whenever you need it:** Move forward with confidence, but know that it's normal to feel lost at some points in the journey. When this happens, don't hesitate to seek support.
:::

The platform is designed for you to build quickly and intelligently.

You **will not need to spend any money** to learn or use the technologies promoted and taught by Netuno. Our entire ecosystem and the tools we recommend are free.
